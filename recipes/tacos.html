Tacos!!

Description

Tacos are staple of mexican culture and the best things that ever existed.

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder meat
  • Pastor seasoning
  • Cilantro
  • Onions
  • Limes
  • Tortillas
  1. First cube into small pieces the shoulder meat and add seasoning as well.
  2. Dice cilantro and onions and slice the limes.
  3. Warm Tortillas on a skillet
  4. Prepare tacos and garnish with veggies and lime and salsa to taste.
